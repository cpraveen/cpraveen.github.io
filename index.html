Research

My primary research interest is CFD and its applications. I am also interested in numerical analysis and numerical solution of PDE in general.

  • Computational Fluid Dynamics
  • Kinetic schemes for compressible flows
  • Meshless method
  • Finite volume method
  • Finite element and Discontinuous Galerkin Method
  • Compressible flows, MHD, Shallow water
  • Well-balanced scheme
  • Adjoint equations and solvers
  • Automatic differentiation
  • Shape optimization
  • Simplex method
  • Particle swarm optimization
  • Metamodelling, surrogate models
  • Parallel computing

Research Projects

A list of research topics, some of which are completed, some semi-completed and others still ongoing.

  1. Discontinuous Galerkin scheme for Euler and MHD equations on adaptive meshes
  2. Positivity preserving Discontinuous Galerkin scheme for ideal MHD
  3. An arbitrary Lagrangian-Eulerian discontinuous Galerkin method for compressible flows
  4. Hybridizable discontinuous Galerkin method for compressible Navier-Stokes
  5. Discontinuous Galerkin method for barotropic vorticity transport equation on the sphere
  6. Discontinuous Galerkin methods for anisotropic diffusion
  7. Finite volume and residual distribution methods for anisotropic diffusion
  8. Discontinuous Galerkin method for shallow water model on the sphere
  9. Finite volume method for compressible RANS on hybrid, unstructured grids
